Nama Dhofar Services — Company Overview

Est. 2001

Company Overview

Dhofar Integrated Services Company S.A.O.C, re-branded as Nama Dhofar Services (NDS), was incorporated in 2001 and is owned by Electricity Holding Company (Nama Holding) by 99%, a fully owned subsidiary of Oman Investment Authority (OIA).

NDS is mandated to provide Electricity Distribution and Supply, Water and Wastewater Services in Dhofar Governorate. The company handles above 0.3 million customer accounts across the three sectors.
